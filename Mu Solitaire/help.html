How to Play

Click a card to select it, click another card to move it.

Cards stack in alternating colors with descending values. Only Kings can be placed on empty spaces.




To win, stack cards of the same suit in the end piles from Ace to King. The game ends when all piles are full.

Scoring

Moving a card to an end pile rewards 10 points. Moving a card from an end pile to the board loses 15 points.

Revealing a card on the board rewards 5 points.







Moving a card from the deck to a board pile rewards 5 points. Each rotation through the deck costs 100 points.







Note that the score will never go below zero.
